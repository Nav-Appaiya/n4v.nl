ZippyTheUnzipper

A small PHP application to unzip larger files on your webhost

PHP

This is a small side project I did a long time ago, when I did not have my own webserver or VPS. I used to have free shared hosting accounts which did not allow me to upload my projects properly. Zippy the Unzipper is a small app that gave me the chance to upload my project through the browser instead of always having to FTP or Terminal in.

It gives you a GUI where you can browse directories and upload you project files with simple upload capabilities. Also this unzips your files directly when you upload, so you dont have to hassle when uploading/unzipping your project.
